About Ayodhya

Ayodhya District is a city located on the bank of river Saryu in Uttar Pradesh India. The city is identified as the legendary city of Ayodhya, and as such, is the birth place of Lord Ram and setting of the epic Ramcharit Manas (Ramayana).
